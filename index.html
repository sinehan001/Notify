Frequently Asked Questions

Explore our comprehensive Frequently Asked Questions to find answers about our web push notification service. Gain insights into customization options, subscription limits, analytics, and support offerings. Simplify your experience and maximize engagement with our platform.

  • What is a web push notification?

    Web push notifications are messages sent by a website or web application to users' browsers, even when the users are not actively using the site. They appear as small pop-up notifications and are a powerful tool for engaging and re-engaging users.

  • How do I enable web push notifications?

    Enabling web push notifications is simple. When you visit a website that supports push notifications, you will be prompted to allow or block notifications. Simply click "Allow," and you'll start receiving notifications from that site.

  • Is there a limit on the number of subscribers in a plan?

    Each plan has a different limit on the number of subscribers. Check the details of each plan in our pricing section to find the subscription limit that best fits your needs.

  • How can I track the performance of my notifications?

    Our platform provides analytics tools that allow you to track the performance of your web push notifications. Monitor click-through rates, delivery rates, and other metrics to optimize your notification strategy.

  • What support options are available?

    Support options vary by plan. Free plans typically include email support, while premium plans may offer priority support and additional assistance channels. Check the details of each plan for specific support offerings.

  • Can I schedule notifications for specific times?

    Yes! For Premium Users, our platform allows you to schedule notifications for specific dates and times, ensuring timely delivery and maximum impact for your audience.

  • Is there a limit on the length of a notification message?

    While there is a character limit for each notification, our platform is designed to accommodate concise and impactful messages to engage your audience effectively.
